# Benchmarks: How Defense Hardware Startups Structure Teams (20 → 100+)

Research brief for the Reorg Studio overview page. Source material from Contrary Research, company pages, Built In, Levels.fyi, press coverage.

| Company | Founded | Peak HC | Key structural signal |
|---------|---------|---------|----------------------|

## Cross-Company Patterns

- Founding triangle: mission/government + systems engineering + operations
- Engineer ratio: 70–80% at 20, ~60% at 50, 40–50% at 100+
- Autonomy/AI always separates from embedded/software
- Quality splits: I&T in Engineering, Production Quality in Manufacturing (50–80 heads)
- Manufacturing starts co-located with R&D, separates when volume forces it
- DC office appears disproportionately early
- Advisory boards substitute for in-house DoD expertise until ~100
